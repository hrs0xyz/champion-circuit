━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FLOW A — Player plays a match and earns points:

  User books slot at Himanshu Gaming Club (Cricket)
       ↓
  Plays match
       ↓
  Venue staff records match on Match Controller
  (adds all players, sets scores, team A vs B)
       ↓
  Admin reviews and verifies match
       ↓
  Points auto-calculated per formula
  and written to match_participants.points_earned
       ↓
  Leaderboard snapshot recomputed (nightly job)
       ↓
  User sees updated rank on /leaderboard
       ↓
  User gets notification: "+10 points from Cricket match"


FLOW B — External traffic → Voucher purchase (Phase 3):

  Partner shares link: championcircuit.com/vouchers?ref=TOKEN
       ↓
  User (not logged in) lands on voucher page
  (city auto-filtered to partner's city)
       ↓
  Clicks "Buy now" → guest checkout
  (enters email + phone only)
       ↓
  Pays via Razorpay (UPI/card/wallet)
       ↓
  Code + QR shown on screen
  Email sent to buyer
       ↓
  User visits venue, shows QR
  Venue scans → mark redeemed ✓
       ↓
  User later creates account → voucher
  linked to their profile via email match


FLOW C — Tournament journey:

  Admin creates tournament
  (or Venue creates → Admin approves)
       ↓
  Status: Registration Open
       ↓
  Players register (solo or team)
  Pay entry fee if required
       ↓
  Status: Live
       ↓
  Matches played, venue records scores
  Admin verifies each match
       ↓
  Status: Completed
       ↓
  Admin sets final positions
  Prize amounts recorded
       ↓
  Points awarded to all participants
  (registration pts + round pts + placement pts)
       ↓
  Leaderboard updated
  Winners notified

  LEADERBOARD POINTS FORMULA (draft)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Base:
    Tournament registration   →  1 pt
    Casual match win          →  3 pts
    Ranked match win          → 10 pts
    Tournament round win      → 15 pts
    Draw                      →  2 pts
    Loss (participation)      →  1 pt

  Placement bonus (tournament):
    1st place                 → 50 pts
    2nd place                 → 30 pts
    3rd place                 → 15 pts

  Multipliers:
    × 1.5  verified venue match
    × 1.2  opponent ranked higher than you (upset)
    × 0.5  casual (unranked) match

  Decay (keeps leaderboard fresh):
    Points older than 90 days → × 0.8 weight in monthly board
    All-time board has no decay
